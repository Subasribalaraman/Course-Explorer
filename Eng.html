ENGINEERING

WHAT IS ENGINEERIG

Engineering is the practical application of scientific principles to solve problems and create new or improved solutions. It involves using knowledge of mathematics and science to design, develop, and build things like machines, structures, and processes. Engineers work to make advancements in various fields, from transportation and construction to technology and medicine, improving our lives through innovation and practical solutions.


POPULAR BRANCHES


Compuer Sceience and engineering(CSE)

Computer Science Engineering (CSE) is a field of study that combines the theoretical aspects of computer science with the practical application of engineering principles to design and build computing systems. It's a popular engineering discipline that prepares graduates for a wide range of careers in the tech industry, focusing on both hardware and software aspects.... to know more about this

Mechanical Engineering

Mechanical engineering is a broad field that involves the design, analysis, and manufacturing of mechanical systems, encompassing everything from small devices to large machinery. It focuses on the principles of physics, mathematics, and materials science to create functional and reliable machines, engines, and systems used in various industries, including transportation, manufacturing, power generation, and medical devices.

Electrical Engineering

Electrical engineering is a branch of engineering that focuses on the study, design, and application of electrical systems, including electricity, electronics, and electromagnetism. It involves understanding how electricity works and using that knowledge to create various technologies. This field has been instrumental in developing technologies like universal electric power, telecommunications, radio, and television, transforming society

Civil Engineering

Civil engineering is a field dedicated to designing, constructing, and maintaining the built environment, including structures like roads, bridges, buildings, and water systems. It encompasses a broad range of sub-disciplines, including structural, geotechnical, transportation, and environmental engineering, all focused on ensuring the safety and functionality of infrastructure

Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)

Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) is an engineering discipline focused on the design, development, and testing of electronic devices and communication systems. It encompasses various aspects, including analog and digital communication, signal processing, antenna and wave propagation, and circuit design

Artificial Intelligence & Data Science (emerging field)

Artificial Intelligence and Data Science is a new branch of study that deals with scientific methodologies, processes, and techniques drawn from different domains like statistics, cognitive science, and computing and information science to extract knowledge from structured data and unstructured data.
